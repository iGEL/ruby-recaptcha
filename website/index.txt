<pre syntax="ruby">sudo gem install recaptcha</pre>

h2. The basics
The ReCaptchaClient abstracts the ReCaptcha API for use in Rails Applications


h2. Demonstration of usage

First, create an account at ReCaptcha.net.

Then, require the gem into your application.rb, and <code>include ReCaptcha::AppHelper</code>. This will mix in validate_recap to your application controller.


Then, in your concrete controller, chain validate_recap into your validation:
<pre>
  def create
      @user = User.new(params[:user])
      if validate_recap(params, @user.errors) && @user.save
             ...do stuff...
</pre>

In your application_helper, <code>include ReCaptcha::ViewHelper</code>. This will mix in get_captcha().

Now you can just call <code>get_captcha()</code> in your view to insert the requisite widget from ReCaptcha
